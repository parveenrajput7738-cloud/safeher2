Got Questions?

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about TripSister. Can't find your answer? Reach out to us.

Is TripSister really only for women? +
Yes, TripSister is an exclusively women-only platform. All members are verified as women through our identity verification process. This is core to creating the safe, trusting environment we promise.
How does the verification process work? +
After signup, you'll be asked to submit a government-issued ID and take a real-time selfie for liveness detection. Our team reviews each submission within 24 hours. Verified members receive a checkmark badge.
Is TripSister free to use? +
Basic membership is completely free — create a profile, search partners, and send up to 5 messages per day. TripSister Pro (coming soon) offers unlimited messaging, priority search, and exclusive travel deals.
What happens if I feel unsafe? +
Use the in-app SOS button to alert your emergency contacts and our 24/7 safety team. You can also block and report any user instantly. We take every report seriously and act within hours.
Can I travel internationally with a TripSister match? +
Absolutely! TripSister is designed for both domestic and international travel. You can search partners by country, city, or even specific destinations, and filter by language spoken and travel style.
How are travel partners matched? +
Our matching algorithm considers destination overlap, travel dates, travel style, interests, language, and reviews from previous travel partners. You can browse matches and initiate contact with anyone who looks like a good fit.
