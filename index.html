Our Story

Got Questions?

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about HerVoyanta. Can't find your answer? Reach out to us.

Is HerVoyanta really only for women? +
Yes, HerVoyanta is an exclusively women-only platform. All members are verified as women through our identity verification process.
How does the verification process work? +
After signup, you'll be asked to submit a government-issued ID and take a real-time selfie for liveness detection. Our team reviews each submission within 24 hours.
Is HerVoyanta free to use? +
Basic membership is completely free — create a profile, search partners, and send up to 5 messages per day. HerVoyanta Pro (coming soon) offers unlimited messaging and priority search.
What happens if I feel unsafe? +
Use the in-app SOS button to alert your emergency contacts and our 24/7 safety team. You can also block and report any user instantly.
Can I travel internationally with a HerVoyanta match? +
Absolutely! HerVoyanta is designed for both domestic and international travel. Search partners by country, city, or specific destinations.
How are travel partners matched? +
Our matching algorithm considers destination overlap, travel dates, travel style, interests, language, and reviews from previous travel partners.
